# Your logo goes here

Put your logo in this folder as **`logo.png`**.

- PNG; **white-on-transparent** looks best, but plain **dark-on-white** art also works.
- It's fit-centered into the dial's logo slot (below the 12) automatically — any aspect ratio.
- No `logo.png`? The face builds with an **empty** slot.

`logo.png` is git-ignored, so your logo never gets committed.
